My story

 

Hi there! I'm Shamim, the creator of Stories and this is my story. I have always wanted to write a book, I even wrote three chapters of one but I gave up because I wasn't sure it was good enough only because I didn't receive any feedback.

Then one day I decided to follow my dream and create something wonderful and I came up with the idea of interactive story telling. A place that nobodies can tell their stories and others listen, vote and interact. Because community is everything!
